# PulseGrid — Demo Script

## 2-3 Minute Demo Narration

### 0:00 — Landing Page (15s)
"Future cities are only smart if they help people respond quickly to real local problems. Today, communities lose critical time when signals are fragmented. PulseGrid solves this."

*Click "Try Demo"*

### 0:15 — Scenario Selection (15s)
"We have three preset scenarios. Let's start with the Heatwave + Water Shortage scenario—a common urban crisis."

*Click the first scenario card*

### 0:30 — Issue Board (30s)
"PulseGrid instantly detects and ranks 5 issues. Notice the water outage is ranked #1 with a score of 88—not because it's the most dramatic, but because it has the highest cascading risk during extreme heat. The prioritization engine scores 8 weighted factors."

*Scroll through the issue list, click the top issue*

### 1:00 — Issue Detail (30s)
"Here's the AI analysis: why it matters, who's affected, and what happens if we don't act. The water outage affects 8,200 households and the pressure is dropping toward full failure."

*Show the detail sidebar with AI enrichment*

### 1:30 — Response Plan (30s)
"Now let's see the response plan. Immediate action: deploy water tankers within 1 hour. The plan includes team assignments, required resources, a 30-minute action plan, and resident instructions."

*Click "View Full Response Plan", show the plan tabs*

### 2:00 — Ask PulseGrid (20s)
"Let's ask: 'Why is the water outage ranked above the traffic issue?' PulseGrid answers from the actual data."

*Type the question, show the response*

### 2:20 — Impact Summary (20s)
"Finally, here's the impact summary: top 3 priorities, affected groups, readiness level, and action coverage. PulseGrid helped us make decisions 30-45 minutes faster than manual assessment."

*Show the summary screen*

### 2:40 — Closing (20s)
"PulseGrid turns scattered urban disruptions into prioritized, actionable response plans. Faster decisions. Better outcomes. More resilient communities."

*End on the summary screen*
